Gravity

The universal gravitation law states that all matter, no matter the size, attracts every other bit of matter. Every object has a gravitational pull. When an object has a large mass and is very dense, it is going to have a stronger gravitational pull. If you have two objects with some mass, they will slowly come together.

  • Gravity equation - g=GM₁/r¹
  • Gravitational Constant - G=6.67 x 10⁻¹¹
  • Fg = Force of gravity between two objects. Both will feel this
  • g = Acceleration due to gravity, constant regardless of m2
  • G = Gravitational Constant, a description of gravity for the universe
  • M1m2 = The two masses, M1 is usually the larger mass
  • R2 = The distance between our two masses, squared
